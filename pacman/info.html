Welcome to MMS 2017!

Now that you've made it to Minneapolis, it's time to take your rightful place as the MMS Pac-Man Champion. In order to do so, you need to track your rivals and know when to be where. You've come to the right place!

On this site (thoughtfully developed by the Adaptiva Design Lab), you'll find the leaderboard that shows all player scores and the current champion, a list of prizes, and some free content and resources we hope you'll enjoy.

Schedule {% include html/collapse-icon.html %}

Other than the final championship, use of the Pac-Man system is managed on a first come first serve basis. Just come to our booth and snag a spot in line when you're ready to play!

The top four ranking gamers will be invited to face off for a $100 Amazon Gift Card and a champion's plaque.

The final championship will be held on Wednesday, May 17th at 3:00 PM Central Time.

Rules & Scoring {% include html/collapse-icon.html %}

We'll keep it simple:

Rules

  1. There's no minimum number of people required to play, but scores are only recorded for games of 2-4 players. In the case of long lines at the machines, solo gamers and smaller groups will be asked to play in groups of up to four.
  2. There's no limit to how many times you can play! Just return to the line after your round is over and scores are reported.
  3. No teams. Sorry!
  4. Be nice to each other!

Scoring

  1. Only the winning score from games of two or more players will be recorded. If you are the winner of a multiplayer round, report your name and score to Adaptiva staff at the booth table.
  2. There are no restrictions on player names on the leaderboard, but please help us out and try to keep it simple.

    By default, we will enter first name and last initial. If you have a gamer tag you'd like to use, we'll be happy to comply unless it's, like, 38 characters long.

    No inappropriate player names, please.
