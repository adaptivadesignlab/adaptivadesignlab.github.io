Rules & Scoring {% include html/collapse-icon.html %}

We'll keep it simple:

Rules

  1. There's is a minimum of two players to play. In the case of long lines at the machines, solo gamers and smaller groups will be asked to play in groups of up to four.
  2. There's no limit to how many times you can play! Just return to the line after all five rounds are over and scores are reported. However, Caribou Coffee gift cards are limited to three per player throughout the conference.
  3. No teams — this is a free-for-all.
  4. Be nice to each other!

Scoring

  1. Only the winner from each round of two or more players will be recorded.
  2. Each game consists of five (5) rounds, and players receive 100 ranking points per round win.
  3. After the game, report your name and number of rounds won to Adaptiva staff at the booth table.
  4. There are no restrictions on player names on the leaderboard, but please help us out and try to keep it simple.

    By default, we will enter first and last name. If you have a gamer tag you'd like to use, we'll be happy to comply unless it's, like, 38 characters long.

    No inappropriate player names, please.
  5. The player with the most round wins at the end of each game will receive a $5 Caribou Coffee gift card, limit three per person throughout the show.
